Project: AscentCustoms

Crafting the Next Generation of 3D Printed Mice

AscentCustoms is a passion project born from a desire to push the boundaries of personalized gaming peripherals. The core mission is to design and develop high-performance, ergonomic, and customizable 3D printed mice tailored to individual user preferences and play styles.

The Vision

In a market often dominated by one-size-fits-all solutions, AscentCustoms aims to provide a truly bespoke experience. By leveraging the flexibility of 3D printing, we can iterate on designs rapidly, incorporate unique ergonomic features, and offer a level of customization that traditional manufacturing cannot easily achieve. This includes variations in shape, size, weight, button placement, and even material textures.

Key Features & Goals

  • Ergonomic Design: Focus on comfort and performance for extended gaming sessions, with shapes catering to different grip styles (palm, claw, fingertip).
  • Lightweight Construction: Utilizing advanced 3D printing techniques and materials to achieve optimal strength-to-weight ratios.
  • High-Performance Internals: Integrating top-tier sensors, switches, and encoders to ensure competitive-grade performance.
  • Modular Components: Exploring ideas for swappable parts, allowing users to easily customize or upgrade their mouse over time.
  • Community-Driven Development: Gathering feedback from enthusiasts and gamers to refine designs and features.
  • Open Source Inspiration: While designs are proprietary, the project draws inspiration from the open-source hardware community and aims to share knowledge where appropriate.
